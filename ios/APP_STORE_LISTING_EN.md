## App Name
Snoke

## Subtitle

## Promo Text
Snoke helps you catch cravings early and respond with short, practical actions.

## Description
Snoke is your companion on the path away from smoking.

The app combines daily check-ins with a data-driven craving risk estimate and suggests short interventions at the right moment.

### What you can do with Snoke
- Log cravings and cigarettes in seconds
- Track daily targets and progress
- See likely risk windows for upcoming hours
- Get simple, actionable nudges (for example breathing or short walk prompts)

### Who is Snoke for?
People who want to reduce smoking with structure, consistency, and less pressure.

### Disclaimer
Snoke is not a medical device and does not replace professional medical advice.
